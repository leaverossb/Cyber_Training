What is a Computer

A computer is a device which lets you manipulate data through the ability to store, receive, and process information


There are three main types of computers:

  • Desktops
  • Laptops
  • Tablets

Computers are made up of multiple types of hardware and software that enables the functions and graphics you see on screen
