Welcome to the Cyber Training Portal

The goal of the KOHS Cyber Training Portal is to help any individual learn the basics of networking, cyber hygiene and cyber security. Here is how the site is structured:

  1. Take a SURVEY to see what you know
  2. Complete TRAINING to improve your knowledge
  3. Check your PROFILE to see your progress and how you rank vs other members of the portal
  4. If you need help or have questions, click ABOUT

That's it! pretty simple right?!
